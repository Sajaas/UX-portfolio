Teamwork & the process

The team consisted of members with almost no experience in golf. We worked remotely during this project, with Miro as our virtual whiteboard.

Overall it was exeptional teamwork with regular check-ins, and where everyone in the team trusted each other to contribute with the workload they've been assigned.

As the facilitator I made sure to be prepared for every step of the design sprint, so that I could answer questions and keep us in line with the schedule.
