UX Portfolio

I used Figma to design the prototyp portfolio website, and then implemented it using the skills I picked up during the programming bootcamp I attended for three intense months.

Gather Data
  • Who do I design for?
  • What do they want?
  • Define it!
People looking at my UX portfolio would be potential employers, or HR from companies that accept internship applications. And maybe my grandmother.

They'd want to know what I'm good at, maybe get to know me a bit, and be able to keep in touch (you know I'll call you soon, grandma!).
Design Ideas
  • Minimalistic
  • Easy navigation
  • Connecting
I decided to create this website to show a few projects, present a short description of what I like and my past jobs, and create easy ways to contact me.

I wrote down the important parts of the site and then moved on to prototyping.
Prototyping
  • Figma!
  • Testing
  • Improving
I used the design I came up with and created an interactive prototype using Figma for the first time (I quickly became a fan!).

I showed the prototype to several people, gathered feedback and improved the site accordingly.
