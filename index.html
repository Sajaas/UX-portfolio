About me



I'm an avid gamer, reader and planner

I’ve worked 8 years at a nerdy store, where it’s part of my job to always help the customer to find what they want - even though they might not know what they’re looking for.

I’m passionate about improving and exploring my job, which is why I’ve ended up hosting signings and themed evenings, created different spotlight promotions and overall trying to optimize the store.

I’m always eager to learn which is why I’ve attended several interesting courses and a programming bootcamp. I love a challenge!
