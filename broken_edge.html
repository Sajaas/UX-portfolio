Case Study

Broken Edge

As part of the publishing team at Fast Travel Games, I conducted UX testing for our publishing title Broken Edge, a 1v1 VR fantasy sword dueling game.

I focused on improving the tutorial and onboarding experience through testing and design discussions with the team.

I identified and suggested solutions to various issues including unclear instructions, missing feedback, and VR specific issues such as menu options being outside the peripheral.

IDENTIFYING THE PROBLEMS

Through testing, I identified issues with the tutorials and onboarding.

While players generally understood how to start the game and select menu options, some struggled with both menus and the tutorial information. These issues included:

  • Menu options missed due to being outside the peripheral
  • Accidentally selecting menu options
  • Accidentally moving on in tutorial and missing information
  • Not understanding the win condition
  • Unclear difference between Flow & Awaken
  • Unclear parts of the gauge
SUGGESTED SOLUTIONS

Possible solutions included:

  • Providing more repetition
  • Clearer instructions
  • More contextual feedback
  • Separating the basic and awaken tutorial
  • Combining Awaken & Flow into one move
KEY TAKEAWAYS
  • Players easily miss options outisde their peripheral
  • Players tend to read and understand text less in VR
  • Players may ignore on-screen prompts if they obstruct the view
  • Players need more contextual feedback and reminders
